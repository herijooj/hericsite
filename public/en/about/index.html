About me

My name is Heric Camargo. I’m a Computer Science student at the Federal University of Paraná.

I was part of the UFPR Meteorology Laboratory as an undergrad research fellow. I currently work as a systems administrator at the C3SL (Center for Scientific Computing and Free Software).

I enjoy computing, automating things, and tweaking setups until they break—then fixing them again.
